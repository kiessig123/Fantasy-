What is Fantasy Hold'em?

Fantasy Hold'em is a fresh new way to enhance your fantasy football experience. Fantasy Hold'em incorporates everything you love about fantasy football with the excitement and thrill that can only come from a great game of poker. Fantasy Hold'em offers the best of both worlds and it does so in a way that is incredibly simple, while being fun and providing a challenge at the same time. "Should I call or Fold!? I think I will have the best "hand" but I am not sure if the risk/reward is worth it. I wish I did not have to wait until Monday night to see Peyton Manning's performance!" Fantasy Hold'em is a game that will be enjoyed by the Hardcore fantasy nerds, and fantasy first timers alike.
